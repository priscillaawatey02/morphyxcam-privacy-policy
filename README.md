# morphyxcam-privacy-policy
Privacy Policy for MorphyxCam
Last updated: November 2025

MorphyxCam is a browser-based photo tool that applies real-time WebGL filters to images captured by your device's camera. This privacy policy explains how MorphyxCam handles user data.

1. Information We Collect: MorphyxCam does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The extension operates entirely within the user’s browser.
All processing occurs locally on your device.

2. Camera Access: MorphyxCam requests permission to access your device’s camera only so it can display and transform the live video feed.
Camera data is never sent to any server. Camera data is never stored. Camera data is never shared with third parties. All image processing happens locally in the browser. You may revoke camera permission at any time through your browser settings.

3. MorphyxCam does not: Track users, Use analytics, Store photos, Upload images, Log usage behavior, Use cookies, or Communicate with external servers. The extension works completely offline after installation.

4. Third-Party Sharing: MorphyxCam does not share any data with third parties, advertisers, or external services.

5. Children’s Privacy: MorphyxCam does not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children or any users.

6. Changes to This Policy: If MorphyxCam is updated in the future to include new functionality, this privacy policy may be updated accordingly. Updates will appear on the same publicly accessible URL.
